Applying to CalFresh

CalFresh helps you buy food. This guide will help you in applying. However, if any of the steps don't fit your situation, call a Calfresh worker to check what you should do!

We will start with what you need, and then go through the steps involved in applying ot CalFresh.

Jump to steps:

Apply

The easiest way to apply is online at My Benefits CalWin. This part will take about 30 minutes. Here's how:

  1. Get an email address (click here for a free one)
  2. Create a My Benefits CalWin account
  3. Sign in and Apply for Benefits

    4. Click Apply for Benefits at the bottom of the page. Follow the screens until you get to the People section where you enter your own information.

  4. Fill out your personal information

    5. If possible, include your name, home address, phone number, and email address.

  5. Click next and Submit!

    6. It will say you are only 4% done, but that's okay. We'll get them the information during the interview.

  6. Write down your confirmation number

    7. You'll need it for Step 3, so please keep track of it.

Things you need for this step

Send documents

Now we're going to scan and email your documents to our office:

Scan or take pictures of these documents using your phone. Put them into one big email, and send the email to: food@sfgov.org

In the body of the email, you can say:

Hello,
I have attached my verification documents for my CalFresh application.
My name is [PUT NAME HERE]
My confirmation number is [PUT CONFIRMATION NUMBER HERE]

Interview

After submitting your application and emailing your documents, you will get a phone call to schedule a time for your interview. The interview will take about one hour can be in person or over the phone. It is your choice.

Make sure you bring your documents from the last step to your interview.

At the end of the interview, we will tell you if you qualify for CalFresh or if we need more information.

Identification

If you don't have a drivers license, that's okay. You can bring any ONE of these instead:

  • Driver's license
  • Voter registration card
  • School records
