Applying for CalFresh

Calfresh helps you buy food, and we want to help you apply for CalFresh

Applying can be confusing, so we created this guide to walk you through applying for CalFresh. We've tried to make the process understandable, but let us know if you have any problems! You can call the CalFresh Call Center at (415) 558-1001 or email us at food@sfgov.org.

Step 1: Gather your documents

To approve your application, we will need some information about you and your household. There are a few categories of information; we've listed the categories here, with some suggestions of documents to verify your information. We may ask you for more documents if we need more information, but you should ask for the reason if we ask for anything beyond the items below.

  • (?)
  • Your basic information
  • Monthly income from all sources
  • Monthly rent, utilities, and other bills

Step 2: Apply

There are three ways to apply for CalFresh: in person, online, or by phone. Applying online is usually the fastest - you can use My Benefits CalWin to apply online in 30 minutes.

  1. You need an email address to use My Benefits CalWin. Don't have one?

  2. Create a My Benefits CalWin account
  3. Sign in and Apply for Benefits
    • Click Apply for Benefits at the bottom of the page. MyBenefits CalWin will take you through the application. You only need to enter the required information.
    • If you are applying for Expedited CalFresh, you will need to enter information about your income.
    Important! Write down your confirmation number. You'll need it for Step 3.
Things you need for this step
  • Social Security Numbers (SSNs) 

Step 3: Send documents

Now we're going to scan and email your documents to our office:

Scan or take pictures of these documents using your phone. Put them into one big email, and send the email to: food@sfgov.org

In the body of the email, you can say:

Hello,
I have attached my verification documents for my CalFresh application.
My name is [PUT NAME HERE]
My confirmation number is [PUT CONFIRMATION NUMBER HERE]
Things you need for this step
  • An ID
  • Proof that you live in San Francisco
  • Proof of all income for the past 30 days

Step 4: Interview

After submitting your application and emailing your documents, you will get a phone call to schedule a time for your interview. The interview will take about 15 minutes to one hour. It can be in person or over the phone. It is your choice.

Be sure to have your documents for the interview.

At the end of the interview, we will tell you if you qualify for CalFresh or if we need more information.

Important! We are required to decide on your case within 30 days. If you haven't heard from us by then, call the CalFresh Call Center at (415) 558-1001.
Things you need for this step
  • An ID
  • Proof that you live in San Francisco
  • Proof of all income for the past 30 days
  • Social Security Numbers (SSNs)

Questions

If this page doesn't answer your questions, give us a call.

(877) 366-3076

Who should I include in the application?

Everyone who lives in your house, who you purchase and prepare food with.

Do you really need the Social Security Number of everyone in my house?

Yes, as long as you purchase and prepare food with them. If someone is not a citizen or Legal Permanent resident, you can leave them off the application.

I'm a veteran. Does this change anything?

No. Just apply as normal.

I'm receiving Supplemental Security Income (SSI). Should I still apply?

No. You aren't eligible if you're receiving SSI. Make sure you are actually receiving SSI and not some other form of income from the Social Security Agency.

I'm not a US citizen or Legal Permanent Resident. Should I still apply?

No. You have to be a citizen or Legal Permanent Resident to get CalFresh. However, if you have a US born child, you can apply for them.

I'm an undocumented immigrant with a US born child. Can I apply for my child?

Yes. Just leave yourself off the application.

I have a Green Card. Should I still apply?

Yes.

Does it matter if I have money in a savings account?

No. We don't look at how much money you have saved up.

After I apply, what happens next?

Someone will call you on the phone to schedule a time for the interview.

Can I do the interview over the phone?

Both! We can do the interview over the phone or in person at 1235 Mission Street, San Francisco.

What if I'm traveling abroad?

If you are traveling or living outside of San Francisco for more than 30 days, you will lose CalFresh.

What holidays are you closed?

New Year’s Day, MLK, President’s Day, Memorial Day, Independence Day, Labor Day, Columbus Day, Veteran’s Day, Thanksgiving and day after, Christmas

How can I bring proof of income if I don't have any income?

Just tell us. We will write this down and ask you to sign.

Identification

If you don't have a drivers license, that's okay. You can bring any ONE of these instead:

  • Birth certificate
  • Driver's license
  • Voter registration card
  • School records
  • U.S. Passport
