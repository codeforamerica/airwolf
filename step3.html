Step 3: Send documents

Now we're going to scan and email your documents to our office:

Scan or take pictures of these documents using your phone. Put them into one big email, and send the email to: food@sfgov.org

In the body of the email, you can say:

Hello,
I have attached my verification documents for my CalFresh application.
My name is [PUT NAME HERE]
My confirmation number is [PUT CONFIRMATION NUMBER HERE]

Identification

  • Driver's license
  • Voter registration card
  • School records
  • U.S. Passport
