Residence in San Francisco

You can show you live in San Francisco by bringing a document that has your name and your address, like one of these:

  • Driver's license
  • Check stub
  • Rent or mortgage receipt
  • Utility bill

Identification

If you don't have a drivers license, that's okay. You can bring any ONE of these instead:

    Dated check stubs for the last 30 days Statement from your employer A current benefit check Copies of child support checks Alimony checks Award letters Statement from college financial aid office
